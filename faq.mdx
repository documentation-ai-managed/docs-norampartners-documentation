## What are the eligibility requirements to become a NorAm partner?

You qualify if your business operates in media, consumer electronics, consumer goods, or related sectors needing 3PL fulfillment, asset recovery, kitting, or returns management. Minimum annual volume requirements apply: `<500` units/month for starters, `>5,000` units/month for premium tiers.

## How is pricing structured?

NorAm uses volume-based tiered pricing with no hidden fees. Base rates start at `$0.50` per unit for fulfillment, scaling down for higher volumes. Contracts are flexible: month-to-month for trials, annual for discounts.

| Tier | Monthly Volume | Fulfillment Rate | Kitting Add-on |
|------|----------------|------------------|----------------|
| Starter | `<500` units | `$0.75`/unit | `$0.20`/kit |
| Growth | `500-5,000` units | `$0.50`/unit | `$0.15`/kit |
| Enterprise | `>5,000` units | `$0.35`/unit | `$0.10`/kit |

<Callout kind="tip">
  Request a custom quote by emailing `quotes@noram.partners` with your projected volumes for optimized rates.
</Callout>

<Expandable title="Sample Contract Terms">

    "slaUptime": "99.9%"

## Who do I contact for support?

Use these channels based on your issue urgency.

| Issue Type | Contact Method | Response Time | Details |
|------------|----------------|---------------|---------|
| Technical (API/Portal) | `support@noram.partners` | `<2 hours` | Include order ID and error logs |
| Billing/Pricing | `billing@noram.partners` | `<24 hours` | Attach invoice numbers |
| Operations (Shipping) | Phone: `1-800-NORAM-HELP` | Immediate | `24/7` for urgent delays |
| General Inquiries | Portal Chat | `<1 hour` | Log in at `partners.noram.com` |
